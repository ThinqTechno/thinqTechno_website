About us

Information Technology enables business and business drives IT. Thriving organizations create one unified team from these divergent silos.
THINQTECHNO enables our partners to align their business and Information Technology needs, uniting knowledge, skills, and resources to maintain a competitive advantage.
Lead by a strong, experienced team from the industry with strong values, our organization provides high client focus and innovation, to accelerate our customers’ digital transformation goals for their products and services, with high-quality, on-time, cost-effective services.
We understand the complexities of modern digital hardware, software technologies, and product development life-cycle. From ideation, design, development, testing, and deployment, to operations, support, and sustenance, THINQTECHNO ensures your digital transformation journey remains a hassle-free experience.
At THINQTECHNO, we aim at being committed long term partners in our customers’ evolving business goals.

Who we are

We are a digital transformation consultancy and software development company that provides cutting edge Engineering Solutions, with a deep understanding of Modern Technology and strong expertise in multiple business domains. We are helping start-ups, medium and large enterprise clients untangle complex issues that always emerge during their digital evolution journey.
